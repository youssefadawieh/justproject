Wafi Gourmet History!

Wafi Gourmet brings the tastiest culinary treasures from the kitchens of Lebanon to the KSA. Established since 2001, we aim to be more than just a restaurant but more a collection of gastronomic experiences transported from our home country. Connoisseurs of authentic Lebanese cuisine will find it difficult to ignore the many mouthwatering temptations that we are famous for.
